### [1] [Coverage and Rate Analysis of Follower-Based LEO Satellite Networks: A Stochastic Geometry Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01265)
*Juanjuan Ru,Ruibo Wang,Mohamed-Slim Alouini*

Main category: cs.IT

TL;DR: 基于球形随机几何模型建立领导者-跟随者卫星集群架构的性能评估框架，分析中断概率和平均数据率，证明集群架构优于单星并探索最优部署配置

Motivation: 为缓解LEO巨型星座中的星间干扰和有效载荷限制，提出卫星集群（领导者-跟随者架构）以提高性能并减少干扰，需要评估其性能增益和最优部署配置

Method: 基于球形随机几何模型建立性能评估框架，推导卫星系统中断概率和平均数据率的解析表达式，并提供低复杂度近似方法

Result: 数值结果表明领导者-跟随者架构相比单一领导者卫星具有性能优势，并探索了跟随者卫星的最优部署配置

Conclusion: 球形随机几何模型有效评估卫星集群性能，领导者-跟随者架构能显著提升通信性能，为LEO星座优化部署提供理论依据

Abstract: To mitigate inter-satellite interference and payload limits in LEO mega-constellations, satellite clusters, groups of small cooperative satellites have been proposed to improve performance and reduce interference. The typical configuration divides the cluster into a leader satellite with full processing and control capabilities and multiple simpler follower satellites that assist with coverage and throughput. These clusters enhance coverage and throughput, prompting interest in their performance gains and optimal deployment. Given that the spherical stochastic geometry (SG) model has been proven effective for modeling such structures, we establish a performance evaluation framework based on the SG approach for the leader-follower satellite architecture, enabling an assessment of communication performance under different deployment configurations quantitatively. We derive analytical expressions for the outage probability and average data rate to evaluate the communication performance of the satellite system, along with low-complexity approximations. Numerical results demonstrate the performance advantages of the leader-follower architecture over a single leader satellite and explore optimal deployment configurations for the follower satellites.

### [2] [On the existence of linear rank-metric intersecting codes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02004)
*Martino Borello,Olga Polverino,Ferdinando Zullo*

Main category: cs.IT

TL;DR: 本文研究了秩度量相交码的参数约束，证明了n≤2m-⌊(k+4)/2⌋，并发现当k=3且m≥6时，上界n=2m-3可达。通过几何解释，将存在性问题归结为散射子空间的存在性，并解决了Bartoli等人提出的开放问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 秩度量相交码是编码理论中的新对象，虽然定义形式与汉明度量相似，但其结构和参数约束表现出显著不同。先前研究表明非退化秩度量相交码必须满足2k-1≤n≤2m-3，但上界的紧致性尚未解决。本文旨在深入探究秩度量相交码的参数约束和几何特性。

Method: 利用秩度量码的q-系统几何解释，证明秩度量相交码的对偶子空间必须满足强规避性质。通过这种几何连接，推导出新的参数限制，并分析极值码的几何特征，将其存在性问题归结为散射子空间的存在性。

Result: 证明了n≤2m-⌊(k+4)/2⌋，并发现当k=3且m≥6时，上界n=2m-3可达。将[2m-3,3,d]_{q^m/q}秩度量相交码的存在性归结为维数为m+3的散射子空间的存在性。利用已知的最大散射子空间构造，在m为偶数时得到存在性结果。证明了[6,3,3]_{q^5/q}秩度量相交码对任意素数幂q都不存在。

Conclusion: 本文通过几何方法深入分析了秩度量相交码的参数约束，建立了更精确的上界，并将存在性问题与散射子空间理论联系起来。解决了Bartoli等人提出的开放问题，为秩度量相交码的进一步研究提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Intersecting codes are a classical object in coding theory whose rank-metric analogue has recently been introduced. Although the definition formally parallels the Hamming-metric case, the structure and parameter constraints of rank-metric intersecting codes exhibit substantially different behavior. It was previously shown that a nondegenerate $[n,k,d]_{q^m/q}$ rank-metric intersecting code must satisfy $2k-1 \le n \le 2m-3$, and the tightness of the upper bound was left open. Using the geometric interpretation of rank-metric codes via $q$-systems, we prove that the dual subspace associated with a rank-metric intersecting code must satisfy strong evasiveness properties. This connection allows us to derive new restrictions on the parameters of such codes and to show that the bound $n=2m-3$ can be attained only when $k=3$ and $m\ge 6$. More generally, we show that $n \leq 2m-\lfloor(k+4)/2\rfloor$. Moreover, we obtain a geometric characterization of these extremal codes in terms of scattered $\mathbb{F}_q$-subspaces of $\mathbb{F}_{q^m}^3$. As a consequence, the existence problem for $[2m-3,3,d]_{q^m/q}$ rank-metric intersecting codes is reduced to the existence of scattered subspaces of dimension $m+3$. Using known constructions of maximum scattered subspaces, we derive existence results when $m$ is even. Finally, we prove that $[6,3,3]_{q^5/q}$ rank-metric intersecting codes do not exist for any prime power $q$, thus resolving an open problem posed by Bartoli et al. in 2025.
