TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于率失真理论的随机Chase译码方法，用信息论最优的比特翻转规则替代传统启发式规则，适用于二进制记忆对称信道上的代数码译码。

Motivation: 传统随机Chase译码中比特翻转概率的确定依赖启发式方法，缺乏理论依据。作者希望用信息论（率失真理论）为翻转规则提供理论基础，从而提升译码性能。

Method: 将随机Chase译码重新解释为错误模式覆盖码的随机编码构造，基于Nguyen等人的率失真框架，针对BMS信道设计比特翻转概率。通过理论推导给出渐近最优翻转规则的显式表达式，并计算保证以高概率译码成功的期望列表大小。

Result: 给出了BMS信道上渐近最优比特翻转规则的显式刻画；在二元和四元对称信道上，通过穷举搜索得到的最优翻转规则与信息论规则在短码长下高度吻合。

Conclusion: 该工作为随机Chase译码提供了信息论基础，证明率失真框架可以导出理论上最优且实际可用的翻转规则，在短码长下也表现良好。

Abstract: This work develops a rate-distortion-based approach to stochastic Chase decoding of algebraic codes over binary memoryless symmetric (BMS) channels, replacing the heuristics traditionally used to determine flip probabilities with information-theoretically grounded flipping rules. In particular, we reinterpret stochastic Chase decoding as a random-coding construction for error-pattern covering codes. Our approach builds on the framework of Nguyen et al., who introduced a rate-distortion formulation of multiple-attempt decoding for Reed-Solomon codes over nonbinary channels. In their formulation, erasure patterns are generated so as to align with, and thereby mask, hard-decision errors. We adapt this framework to the design of bit-flip probabilities for Chase decoding over BMS channels. This yields an explicit characterization of the asymptotically optimal bit-flipping rule, together with the expected list size required to ensure that the transmitted codeword appears in the decoding list with high probability. Moreover, for binary and quaternary symmetric channels, we demonstrate that the optimal bit-flipping rule, determined by exhaustive search, closely matches the information-theoretic rule even at short block lengths.
